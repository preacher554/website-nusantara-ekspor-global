Strict Quality Control

Every shipment is checked before loading.

  • Raw material selection, cleaning, sorting, and size grading before packing.
  • Moisture level, product condition, packaging, and warehouse storage control.
  • Final inspection before loading with shipment documentation review.
  • Commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and supporting certificates when required.
